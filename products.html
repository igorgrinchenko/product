Products

EVA ICS

Open-source platform with unique features for Industrial and Home IoT EVA ICS is a platform for automated control and monitoring systems development, for any needs, from home/office to industrial setups. It is completely free for non-commercial use as well as for commercial, on condition that enterprise integrates it on its own. The product is distributed as a free software and is available under Apache License 2.0.

PS/RT

Server developed especially for industrial applications, such as EVA ICS: low latencies, secure model, ability to deal with slow channels far from urban areas.

EVAICS BOX

EVAICS BOX is a powerful mini computer for all kinds of
automation. It is based on Raspberry Pi compute module 4 and can have up to 8 GB of RAM and up to 32 GB of eMMC

BUS/RT

BUS/RT is a rust-native IPC broker, written in Rust/Tokio, inspired by zeromq and nanomsg.
